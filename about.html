    ABOUT US

    Our Story

    At Fındık Ocağı, we are passionate about delivering the finest hazelnuts straight from our fields to your table. Establihed in 2019, nestled in the heart of Sakarya collabrating with local farmers and producers by offering comprehensive processing services, including cracking, roasting, and packaging. By handling hazelnuts from other producers, we help them prepare their products with precision and care, ensuring every batch meets the highest standards of freshness, flavor, and nutritional value. Using advanced machinery and adhering to strict quality standards, we take pride in supporting our community while preserving the rich heritage of Türkiye, which proudly produces approximately 70% of the world’s hazelnut supply.

    Our Vision

    As we look to the future, we aim to expand our reach, bringing the richness of Türkiye’s hazelnuts to even more people worldwide. Whether through our own production or by supporting other farmers with exceptional processing services, we are committed to maintaining the quality, authenticity, and innovation that define FINDIK OCAĞI. Our goal is to create a thriving network of hazelnut producers and consumers, ensuring that everyone can experience the excellence of our products.

    Our Values

    Quality: Every product and service is crafted with care and precision.
    Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly practices to protect our planet and support local farmers.
    Community: We value the hardworking farmers and producers who contribute to our shared success.
    Innovation: By combining tradition with modern methods, we continually strive for improvement.
    Collaboration: Partnering with farmers and producers, we ensure their products meet the highest standards and reach a global market.

    FINDIK OCAĞI

    We specialize in hazelnut processing and product distribution. From hazelnut flours to chopped hazelnuts, cracking, roasting and packaging.
